📦 Pallet Panic
Pallet Panic is a first-person (FPS) asymmetric simulation/roguelike game with a distinct low-poly retro aesthetic, set in the endless, dimly lit corners of a mysterious warehouse.

You play as an average warehouse worker who starts their shift just trying to make ends meet, only to find themselves trapped in a series of nonsensical, eerie, and paranormal anomalies. Keep in mind: This is not a horror game; it's an absurd, tense, and uncanny mystery.

🎮 Gameplay Loop & Mechanics
Order Tracking: Monitor the main terminal for incoming customer requests.

Box & Belt Management: Press the red button to request an empty box, grab the correct items from the pallets, pack them up, and hit the green button to send the box down the line.

Time Pressure: Each day has a strict time limit. If you fail to meet your daily packing quota before the clock runs out, it's game over.

Hardcore Roguelike Punishment: Get 3 strikes (wrong orders) or run out of time, and you're cooked. You lose all progress and start all the way back from Day 1.

Uncanny Mystery: While you focus on your mundane job, shifting objects, strange lights, and an underlying sense of an "anomalous reality" will keep you on edge.
